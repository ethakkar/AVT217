Good Restaurant Websites

Big Buns

Big Buns has a simple homepage with high quality food images and a white background that makes the photos stand out. Although the logo is not in the top left corner, it is centered above the navigation and is apparent due to the white color. They use a grid system to alternate food images with their corresponding descriptions. The social media websites and menus for each location are easy to find. The contact form is easy to use.

BurgerFi

The BurgerFi homepage plays a high quality video of the fresh ingredients they pride themselves on using. The navigation bar is simple and limited. The map of locations is easy to find. They use a grid system below the video to highlight food items. Social media websites are the bottom of the homepage with career, franchising, and contact information. The contact form is easy to use and allows not only customer care, but photo release and press inquires among others. The green order links stand out and are easy to find.

Matchbox

Matchbox uses high quality photos of their food on their homepage and breaks up the site between scrolling images and a white background using a grid system. The menus and social media links are easy to find. Each location is listed and allows quick access to information on addresses, hours, menus, private events, and building history. The use of red for online ordering and reservation links makes them stand out.

Alamo Drafthouse

Alamo Drafthouse has social media icons at both the top and bottom of the page. The main photo is an image of food, beer and a cocktail. Right underneath the photo they strategically discuss their award-winning cuisine, craft beers and exceptional cocktails. Visitors can pick different theaters to see individual menus and prices. The text in the navigation bar is larger than most allowing clear and easy access to other pages.

Copperwood Tavern

Copperwood Tavern has high quality scrolling images of locally sourced proteins, fresh oysters and exceptional craft beers. The logo is centered at the top, but your eye is drawn to the center due to the call out box with menu links. Their social media links are located at the top of page making them easy to find. They list each of their locations with addresses at the bottom of the homepage, along with specials. And they have a dog of the month!
