Background

"I am an outdoor recreation enthusiast currently working for the National Park Service in Washington, DC. As an environmental scientist and graphic designer, I lead outreach and communication efforts that promote science, advocacy and activism in a strategic and inclusive manner."

I wasn't one of those people who always knew exactly what I wanted to do with my life.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biology in 2011, with no idea what the future would hold. In 2012, I began volunteering for the National Park Service and at Rock Creek Park in Washington, DC and found my calling.

Since that time, I have worked in multiple parks from regional to state to federal. I have an obsession with environmental education and documenting nature through photography. This has led me to pursue graphic design and work to develop environmental and park based interpretive media.
