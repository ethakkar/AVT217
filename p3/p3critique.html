Good Portfolio Websites

Matthew Park

Matthew Park utilized a grid system to display the breadth of his work on his portfolio home page. Every tile image provides a link to a new page where he puts the work into context describing the problem, his role, the users, and the design process along with providing more images. He also included non-client work and a link to his resume.

Amelia Abromaitis

It is obvious looking at the portfolio home page for Amelia Abromaitis that she has experience in strategic communications. Examples of her work are front and center, broken out by category. She included appropriate examples that highlighted her work on political and community campaigns.

Lisa Michele Burns

Lisa Michele Burns portfolio website displays her photography in a grid system. The navigation allows the visitor to view images in her portfolio based on category. She lists her clients and partners and provides links to her media and interviews. The imagery she highlights presents a cohesive theme throughout her portfolio.

Brendan Dowling

Brendan Dowling showcases his projects vertically on the right side of his portfolio website. It makes it easy for visitors to see the variety of clients and projects he has worked on. It is a very simple website that includes a large breadth of work.
